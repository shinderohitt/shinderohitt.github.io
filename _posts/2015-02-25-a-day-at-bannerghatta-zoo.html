It was an awesome day! I hadn't seen most of these animals in my life. Fortunately those Ostriches were behind the bars, they seemed more frightening than the Leopards. We couldn't believe they could be that tall.

But the life they are living is absolutely terrible. I can't imagine myself being locked up, counting my time, a show to the world, all my life. Poor souls. Inhuman humans.
